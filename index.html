Learn How to Give Past-Present-Future Readings

For Absolute Beginners!

Navigate through step-by-step instructions for performing a three-card Past-Present-Future reading. If you are a complete beginner - do not be alarmed! This activity is designed to guide all novice learners through the process of reading.

Be sure to have the following elements present before you start:

  • Perspective Tarot deck
  • Perspective Tarot guidebook
  • Instructional worksheet for performing a Past-Present-Future reading
  • A querent (aka the person asking for the reading!)
